About Us

Green Club Anakapalli is a club of environment lovers. It was established by Mr. K.Phani Bhushan Sridhar(Vriksha Mitra awardee and National level best teacher awardee) in 2015 with the support of his father late Dr. Ramalinga Swamy a well known agricultural scientist. Mr. Sridhar started his journey as a one man army but in a short time he got support from environment lovers and he was encouraged from retired forest ranger Sri B. Vinod Kumar and Karri Sanyasi Naidu as a result now the club is flourishing with nearly 100 members. The main motto of the club is to make an eco-friendly environment. Another goal of the club is aware each and every person about the human relationship between plants and other living creatures like birds, the main slogan of the club is without trees, there is no life, there is no good for mankind. meaning of this is without plants there is no survival of any creature on the earth, especially human beings. why because plants given oxygen for breathing and food for survival and shelter for safe living fr us.

Objectives of Green Club

The main aim of establishing Green Club to conserve the natural resources and environment, to create eco-friendly.

To motivate the students as well as everyone to keep surroundings green and clean.

To promote the practice of reduce reuse, recycle.

To celebrate environmental days like world environment day, forest day, world water day, one day etc.

To organize programmes such as quiz, easy writing, competitions rallies etc regarding various environment issues.
