Who We Are

Passionate About Our Planet

Green Club Anakapalli is a club of environment lovers. It was established by Mr. K.Phani Bhushan Sridhar (Vriksha Mitra awardee and National level best teacher awardee) in 2015 with the support of his father late Dr. Ramalinga Swamy, a well known agricultural scientist.

Mr. Sridhar started his journey as a one man army but in a short time he got support from environment lovers. He was encouraged by retired forest ranger Sri B. Vinod Kumar and Karri Sanyasi Naidu. As a result, now the club is flourishing with nearly 100 members. The main motto of the club is to make an eco-friendly environment and to aware each and every person about the human relationship between plants and other living creatures. The main slogan of the club is: "Without trees, there is no life".

What We Do

Our activities focus on education, preservation, and community building.

Community Gardens

We maintain local plots where we grow organic vegetables and native plants, providing fresh produce for the community and habitat for pollinators.

Recycling Drives

Organizing monthly e-waste and plastic collection events to ensure harmful materials are disposed of correctly and kept out of landfills.

Workshops

Hosting educational sessions on composting, sustainable living, and biodiversity to spread knowledge and inspire change.
