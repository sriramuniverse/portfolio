About Us

Rooted in Purpose, Growing with Passion

Global Awareness

Community Service

Our Initiatives

Projects That Make a Difference

Explore our ongoing initiatives designed to create measurable environmental impact.

Campus Solar Initiative

Advocating for and helping install solar panels on campus buildings to reduce carbon footprint.

42 kW Capacity installed

Waste Audit Program

Conducting regular waste audits to identify reduction opportunities and improve recycling rates.

68% Diversion rate achieved

Water Conservation

Installing rainwater harvesting systems and promoting water-wise practices across campus.

120K L Water saved monthly

Green Transport

Promoting cycling, carpooling, and advocating for better pedestrian infrastructure on campus.

200+ Cyclists registered

Urban Garden Project

Creating and maintaining organic gardens that provide fresh produce and biodiversity habitats.

15 Garden beds active

Eco-Education Series

Developing curriculum and hosting seminars on environmental science for schools and communities.

1,200+ Students reached
